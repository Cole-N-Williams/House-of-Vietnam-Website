Our Mission

House of Vietnam is dedicated to preserving Vietnamese culture and heritage while supporting our community members in their journey toward success and integration.

We provide essential social services, cultural education, and community support to Vietnamese families and individuals. Through our programs, we celebrate our rich traditions while embracing opportunities in our new home.

What We Do

Supporting our community through diverse programs and services

Education Programs

Vietnamese language classes, cultural workshops, and academic support for all ages

Social Services

Immigration assistance, job training, healthcare navigation, and family support services

Cultural Events

Tet celebrations, traditional festivals, and community gatherings throughout the year

Youth Programs

Mentorship, leadership development, and cultural identity programs for young people

Career Development

Job placement assistance, professional skills training, and career counseling services

Senior Services

Health programs, social activities, and support services for Vietnamese seniors
